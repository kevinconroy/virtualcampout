Don't have a tent? Or perfer to campout inside your house? That's great! Let's make an epic pillow fort!

To make an epic pillow fort, you'll need:

* Chairs
* Blankets and sheets
* Lots of pillows
* Open space near a couch
* Clothes pins
* Binder clips
* Heavy books
* <em>Optional:</em>
   * Lights
   * Dowel rods or broomsticks
   * String
   * Tape
